Kshitij Madhav Bhat is a graduate student majoring in Robotic Systems Development at the Robotic Institute, School of Computer Science at CMU. 
He received his bachelor's degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Indore in 2024. Prior to joining CMU, he has gained valuable research 
experience as an intern at IIT Delhi, and Lakehead University. His undergraduate research on deep learning for LiDAR data for autonomous vehicles 
has been published in leading computer vision conferences such as CVPR 2024 and ICIP (workshop) 2024. Through rigorous coursework at CMU and his 
undergraduate research, he has gained expertise in computer vision, perception and control for robotics and autonomous vehicles.
